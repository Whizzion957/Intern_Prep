# Intern At IITR - Beta User Guide

Welcome to the **Intern At IITR** beta! This guide will help you get started with the platform.

---

## 🔐 Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Click **"Login with Channel-i"** on the homepage
2. Authenticate using your IIT Roorkee credentials
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

---

## 📊 Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:
- Quick stats (questions posted, companies added)
- Quick action buttons
- Navigation to all features

---

## 📝 Core Features

### 1. View Interview Questions
Navigate to **Questions** to browse all submitted interview questions.

**Filters available:**
- Search by keyword
- Filter by company
- Filter by question type (Interview, OA, Group Discussion, HR)
- Filter by year
- Sort by date 

### 2. Add Interview Question
Click **"Add Question"** to share your interview experience:

1. **Select Company** - Search and select the company **(or create new)**
2. **Question Type** - Interview, Online Assessment, or Others
3. **Month & Year** - When did the interview happen?
4. **Question** - Describe the question asked (supports rich text formatting)
5. **Suggestions** (optional) - Add helpful tips for future candidates

### 3. Browse Companies
Navigate to **Companies** to explore:
- Company profiles with descriptions
- Internship roles and stipend details
- Eligibility criteria per role
- Discussion tips from other students

---

## 🏢 Company Details

Each company page shows:
- **Company Info**: Logo, name, description
- **Roles**: Different internship positions
- **Compensation**: Stipend breakdown (monthly + one-time benefits)
- **Eligibility**: CGPA requirements, branch restrictions
- **Perks**: Meals, accommodation, travel support, etc.
- **Discussion Tips**: Community-shared insights

### Adding Company Tips
At the bottom of each company page, you can:
1. Share tips about the interview process
2. Reply to existing tips
3. Help build the knowledge base

---

## ✏️ Managing Your Submissions

### My Claims
Access via the user menu → **"My Claims"** to:
- **View all questions you've claimed you are related to so that students can ask doubts to you**
- Edit your submissions
- Track your contributions

### Editing Questions
- Click on any question you've submitted
- Use the edit button to modify details
- Changes are saved immediately

---

## 🔍 Search Tips

### Finding Questions
- Use keywords from the question content
- Filter by specific company for targeted results
- Combine filters for precise searches

### Finding Companies
- Search by company name on the Companies page
- Browse the paginated list (100 companies per page)

---

## 💡 Best Practices

### When Adding Questions
✅ Be specific - include context about the round  
✅ Use formatting for clarity (bullets, bold)  
✅ Add suggestions if you have helpful tips  
✅ Specify the correct month/year

### When Adding Tips
✅ Share genuine insights from your experience  
✅ Be respectful and constructive  
✅ Reply to help clarify others' questions
